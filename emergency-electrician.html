Emergency Electrical Repairs

Electrical emergencies can occur at any time without warning. Whether you have lost power entirely, have an acrid burning plastic smell coming from a consumer unit, notice repeatedly tripping circuit breakers, or encounter exposed live conductors, immediate intervention is essential to safeguard your property and loved ones.

We provide rapid diagnostic fault finding and emergency electrical repairs across Dorset and Hampshire borders for homeowners, landlords, and local businesses. Every out-of-hours response operation is handled safely by qualified engineers and completed in absolute compliance with UK BS7671 electrical wiring standards.

Common Electrical Emergencies We Resolve

🔌

Power Outages

Emergency diagnostics for complete or partial loss of property supply paths.

We track down loose distribution elements, open circuits, or primary component breakdown points to quickly establish temporary bypass lines or stable permanent supply links.
⚡

Tripping Electrics

Diagnosing underlying line leakage faults causing RCDs or breakers to drop.

Using insulation resistance testing gear to map the specific neutral or earth fault avenue, isolating the problem component cleanly without dropping your entire grid infrastructure.
🔥

Burning Smells

Urgent chemical investigation of loose arcing points and melting plastics.

High-resistance terminations generate intense local heat under minimal currents. We drop target circuit loads instantly and install new compliant terminal wiring connections.
🛡️

Faulty Fuse Boards

Emergency mechanical repairs and diagnostic management on consumer units.

Replacing individual failed protective devices, overloads, or compromised main switch sets to maintain absolute building structural safety parameters.
⚠️

Exposed Wiring

Isolating damaged wiring runs and encapsulating exposed copper conductors.

If cables are inadvertently cut during structural changes or panels are broken, we ensure the surrounding conductors are safely made dead and enclosed.
🔍

Advanced Fault Finding

Rapid diagnostic meter analysis across complex underlying circuitry lines.

Utilizing calibrated loop impedance and insulation resistance equipment to track complex open neutral anomalies or hidden short circuits inside wall cavities.

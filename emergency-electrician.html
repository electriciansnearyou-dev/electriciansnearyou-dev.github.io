Emergency Electrician FAQs

How quickly can you attend?

Response times vary depending on location and workload, but we aim to attend emergencies as quickly as possible.

What counts as an electrical emergency?

Burning smells, smoke, loss of power, exposed wiring, electric shocks and dangerous electrical faults should be treated as emergencies.

Should I call if my electrics keep tripping?

Yes. Repeated tripping often indicates a fault that requires investigation by a qualified electrician.

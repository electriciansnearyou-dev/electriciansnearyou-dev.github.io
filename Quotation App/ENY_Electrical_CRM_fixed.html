Wholesaler Bulk Price Review

Export your full stock directory as a CSV to send to a wholesaler for a global price review. They can either fill in the "New Cost" column and send the CSV back, or just send a fresh quote PDF (same STB-style layout as the Smart Import tool) — either format is accepted here. Every matched SKU is overwritten with the new price, whether it went up or down. This is fully independent of the Smart Import tool above (which only ever increases prices and can add new items) and the Team Sync tool in the Vault tab (which is for syncing with colleagues) — neither is affected by this.

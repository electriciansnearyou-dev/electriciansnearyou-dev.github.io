Electricians Covering Poole, Bournemouth, Christchurch & Dorset

ElectriciansNearYou provides professional electrical services across Poole, Bournemouth, Christchurch and surrounding Dorset areas. Whether you need an emergency electrician, fuse board upgrade, EV charger installation, EICR certificate, lighting installation or a complete house rewire, our qualified electricians deliver safe, reliable and fully compliant electrical work for domestic and commercial customers.

We understand that electrical faults can happen at any time, which is why we offer rapid response emergency electrician services throughout Dorset. From tripping electrics and power outages to fault finding and urgent repairs, our experienced team works quickly to identify problems and restore power safely while ensuring all work complies with current BS7671 wiring regulations.

We pride ourselves on transparent pricing, professional workmanship and excellent customer service, providing free quotations and practical electrical solutions tailored to your property and requirements.
